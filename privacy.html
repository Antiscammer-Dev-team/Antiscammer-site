Privacy Policy for AntiScammer

Last Updated: 04/10/25

Welcome to AntiScammer! Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Data We Collect

By using AntiScammer, you acknowledge that the bot collects and stores the following data:

  • Message Content: All messages sent in a server where AntiScammer is active are monitored and recorded.
  • User Information: We log user IDs, usernames, and server IDs for moderation and scam prevention.
  • Ban & Scam Reports: We store ban records and scam-related reports for moderation purposes.

2. How We Use Your Data

The collected data is used strictly for:

  • Detecting and banning scammers and malicious users.
  • Reviewing reported messages to improve scam detection.
  • Maintaining server security and moderation logs.

We do not use this data for advertising, profiling, or non-security-related purposes.

3. Who Has Access to Your Data?

Only one authorized individual has access to stored message logs and reports. Your data is never shared, sold, or made public.

4. Data Retention & Removal

Messages and logs are retained for moderation and security purposes.

Users may request data removal via our Discord: https://discord.gg/BVFsE2Z29Q or by directly messaging Ram2.

5. Security Measures

We implement strict security practices, including:

  • Restricted access (only one authorized individual).
  • Secure data storage to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Regular log reviews to ensure compliance with this policy.

Despite our efforts, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute data protection. In the event of a leak, our support server will be notified immediately.

6. Your Rights

Users may:

  • Request to view or delete their recorded data.
  • Appeal automated bans at https://discord.gg/BVFsE2Z29Q.
  • Remove the bot to stop further data collection.

For data inquiries, contact Ram2.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of AntiScammer means you accept the latest version.

For any questions or concerns, reach out to Ram2 on our support server: https://discord.gg/BVFsE2Z29Q.
